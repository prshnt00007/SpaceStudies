Delta-v: The Rocket Equation

-- km/s

This uses the Tsiolkovsky rocket equation: delta-v = Isp x 9.80665 x ln(m0/mf). The reference bars below are commonly cited approximate delta-v budgets for context, not exact figures for any specific mission. Try lowering the final mass (more propellant burned relative to the rocket) and watch delta-v rise sharply - this is why staging (dropping empty mass) is so valuable in real rockets.
